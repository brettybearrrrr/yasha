About Yasha Carpentier

Decades of clinical expertise dedicated to transformative therapeutic outcomes

Clinical Excellence Through Evidence-Based Practice

Yasha Carpentier is a licensed independent clinical social worker (LICSW) with over 27 years of clinical expertise, providing both telehealth and in-person counseling services in Seattle, Washington.

Her therapeutic approach integrates evidence-based methodologies with extensive clinical experience. Drawing from comprehensive training in multiple therapeutic modalities, Yasha provides individualized treatment plans that address complex emotional and behavioral patterns through proven clinical interventions.

With 20 years of specialized experience in medical social work within pediatric and mental health settings, she brings unique expertise in supporting families navigating medical trauma, chronic illness, and complex life transitions.
